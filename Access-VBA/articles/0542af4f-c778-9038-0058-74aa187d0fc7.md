
# Form.SplitFormPrinting Property (Access)

Gets or sets whether the contents of the form or the datasheet are printed when printing a form displayed in Split Form view. Read/write [AcSplitFormPrinting](9c674eed-2b8b-10fc-c270-b908c99fd474.md).


## Syntax

 _expression_. **SplitFormPrinting**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **Form** object.
