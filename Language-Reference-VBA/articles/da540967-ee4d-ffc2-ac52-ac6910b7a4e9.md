
# Show or hide the grid




1. From the  **Tools** menu, choose **Options**.
    
2. Select the  **General** tab sheet.
    
3. Do one of the following:
    
    
    
      - To show the grid, check the  **Show Grid** box.
    
  - To hide the grid, clear the  **Show Grid** box.
    

    
    
4. Click OK.
