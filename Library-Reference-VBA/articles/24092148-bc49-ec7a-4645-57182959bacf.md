|**msoIodGroupPIAs**|0|PIAs group.|
|**msoIodGroupVSTOR35Mgd**|1|VSTO 3.5 managed group.|
|**msoIodGroupVSTOR40Mgd**|2|VSTO 4.0 managed group.|
