
# TaskItem.MarkComplete Method (Outlook)

Marks the task as completed.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **MarkComplete**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **TaskItem** object.


## Remarks

 Sets **[PercentComplete](39525055-647b-02c0-a9da-150698181511.md)** to "100%", **[Complete](c079d11a-bc69-652d-d9c5-6a525f319686.md)** to **True** , and **[DateCompleted](17e6a4af-4cd9-0c5e-35ab-5232cf067478.md)** to the current date.
