
# HeadersFooters Properties (PowerPoint)

## Properties



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
|[Application](789cb927-7f3d-f04a-99b8-92c98af682da.md)|Returns an  **[Application](978c2b99-4271-b953-4283-73b5f3d96f41.md)** object that represents the creator of the specified object.|
|[DateAndTime](15d8f1a4-c48f-7afd-d701-d5e7545aadd4.md)|Returns a  **[HeaderFooter](8aeafb02-adec-17c1-3108-565c78a64ed1.md)** object that represents the date and time item that appears in the lower-left corner of a slide or in the upper-right corner of a notes page, handout, or outline. Read-only.|
|[DisplayOnTitleSlide](adcf0504-50ce-b302-c61f-08425acf847c.md)|Determines whether the footer, date and time, and slide number appear on the title slide. Applies to slide masters. Read/write. |
|[Footer](a684ae25-7174-50e5-095e-0073873628e6.md)|Returns a  **[HeaderFooter](8aeafb02-adec-17c1-3108-565c78a64ed1.md)** object that represents the footer that appears at the bottom of a slide or in the lower-left corner of a notes page, handout, or outline. Read-only.|
|[Header](83748bf7-10a4-1ee7-4eef-4ef8fd38b7da.md)|Returns a  **[HeaderFooter](8aeafb02-adec-17c1-3108-565c78a64ed1.md)** object that represents the header that appears at the top of a slide or in the upper-left corner of a notes page, handout, or outline. Read-only.|
|[Parent](0168b397-a648-3159-bccf-b8e3530eec8a.md)|Returns the parent object for the specified object.|
|[SlideNumber](c846069f-dd3f-c5ac-f9ac-b5a7ed499bdc.md)|Returns a  **[HeaderFooter](8aeafb02-adec-17c1-3108-565c78a64ed1.md)** object that represents the slide number in the lower-right corner of a slide, or the page number in the lower-right corner of a notes page or a page of a printed handout or outline. Read-only.|
