
# VisSelectMode Enumeration (Visio)

Constants passed to and returned by the  **Selection.IterationMode** property and the **CreateSelection** method.



|**Constant**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **visSelModeOnlySub**|&amp;H0800|Selection reports only subselected shapes.|
| **visSelModeOnlySuper**|&amp;H0200|Selection reports only superselected shapes.|
| **visSelModeSkipSub**|&amp;H0400|Selection does not report subselected shapes.|
| **visSelModeSkipSuper**|&amp;H0100|Selection does not report superselected shapes.|
