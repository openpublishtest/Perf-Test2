
# Line.Width Property (Word)

Returns or sets the width, in points, of a line. Read/write  **Long** .


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **Width**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Line](1fbc9a15-c677-0f79-4311-9e6de6fc1b27.md)** object.
