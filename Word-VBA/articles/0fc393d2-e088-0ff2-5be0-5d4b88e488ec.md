
# Legend.Height Property (Word)

Returns or sets the height, in points, of the object. Read/write  **Double** .


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **Height**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Legend](f0122074-87b7-0225-3c6c-406103fa4c29.md)** object.
