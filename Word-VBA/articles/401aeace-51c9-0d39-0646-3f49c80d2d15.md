
# Floor.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Floor](01d277eb-501b-09e5-65b8-83506c76ac05.md)** object.
