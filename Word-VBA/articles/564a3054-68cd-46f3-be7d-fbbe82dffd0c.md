
# TableOfFigures Properties (Word)

|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|

|[HidePageNumbersInWeb](2a937df2-3f0d-a1e1-e402-3e9b06717fe4.md)|Returns or sets whether page numbers in a table of contents or a table of figures should be hidden when publishing to the Web. Read/write  **Boolean** .|
