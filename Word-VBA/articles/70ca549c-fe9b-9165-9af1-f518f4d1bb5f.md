
# Walls.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Walls](e98c7218-b944-12bb-caf9-daecee4b6c0c.md)** object.
